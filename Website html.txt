<article>
  <h1 style="font-size:200%;color:rgb(0,201,171);font-family:verdana;"> What is ReROUTE?</h1>
  <p style="color:rgb(255,255,255);font-family:verdana;">ReROUTE is a software used to model & simulate real-life metro, subway, and train systems, with an optimized signal system as well as dynamic traffic control. The system is modeled on real life communications based train control systems (an example from the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mjx3S3UjmnA" style="color:rgb(255,255,255);">New York Subway</a>) and inspired by the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yJlsSQBhKgE" style="color:rgb(255,255,255);">ongoing upgrade of the Toronto Transit Commissions Line 1 Signal Upgrades</a>. Our first route to be modeled will as such be TTC Line 1.</p>
  <p style="color:rgb(255,255,255);font-family:verdana;">ReROUTING" to an autonomous future.</p>
</article>


<article>
  <h1 style="font-size:200%;color:rgb(0,201,171);font-family:verdana;">What it Does</h1>
  <p style="color:rgb(255,255,255);font-family:verdana;">ReROUTE is an automated train system. It organizes communications between stations and trains to avoid dangerous encounters, while moving passengers in the most efficient manner possible. ReROUTE ensures that passengers arrive at their station swiftly and safely, guaranteeing an on-time arrival.</p>
</article>

<article>
  <h1 style="font-size:200%;color:rgb(0,201,171);font-family:verdana;">Features</h1>
  <p style="color:rgb(255,255,255);font-family:verdana;">-Real Time Variable Traffic ~An RTC (Real Time Clock) feature simulates peak periods
  <br>
  -Realistic Location Variable Traffic ~Different stations feature varying levels of traffic to match real-life counterparts
  <br>
  -Intelligent Train Dispatch ~More trains can be brought online to manage demand
  <br>
  -Intelligent Train Spacing ~Train spacing can be dependent on speed, desired headway, and track conditions
  <br>
  -Dynamic Speed Control ~Train spacing and speed are interconnected
  </p>
</article>
